23/01/2014

Due to Wizard's accidentally uploading the FAQs for the new website we've seen a huge amount of cards today. The full FAQs can be found here: http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/faq/bng .

I'm loving some of the new blue cards such as the Vortex Elemental and the Perplexing Chimera. The first threatens to take something down with it, an excellent deterant in multiplayer games. If you get the Chimera out you can force your opponent into some difficult decisions. As I read it the steal ability will include all spells including creatures which makes playing a more powerful creature a risky move. It could also be heaps of fun in multiplayer with the Chimera getting passed all over the table.

All rare and mythic cards have now been revealed which leaves only 18 uncommons and 37 common cards left to see!

22/01/2014

Today we get to see a full cycle of commons. The Nyxborn cycle includes a bestow creature from every color. None of these are particularly exciting however, as other than the bestow ability they are all vanilla and none of them appear like they'll be particularlly powerful in any format.

Oracle of Bones is the most interesting card revealed today. Its tribute cost involves your opponent choosing between giving it 2 +1/+1 counters or allowing you to cast a instant or sorcery from your hand for free. As this guy has haste it forces the opponent into an immediate and difficult decision. This could potentially be very powerful in a draft and could even be used in constructed. I love cards that give you the opportunity to bluff and I could see myself holding onto a land to try and gain a few free counters. even be useful in constructed.

There has also been a few website updates today. I've fixed the messages so you get useful messages when your adding cards to your pool in a draft or in the sealed simulator. There are also messages alerting you when you've begun a new draft or how many boosters you've opened in the booster simulator.

21/01/2014

Wizards revealed 2 new rares, one mythic and 2 uncommon cards today. These included the card I was most excited about: Phenax. Unfortunetly, Phenax is a mill card. After attempting to build a mill deck for a while myself I've always found them fairly underwhelming as a main strategy, not to mention uninteresting as the other players actions are largely ignored. However, Phenax would go very nicely in a Commander deck and would work wonders in drafts.

Whelming wave was also revealed today. This sends all creatures back to their owners hands except Krakens, Octopus, Leviathans and Serpants. On the surface this doesn't seem that interesting but it does suggest there is a potential for more giant sea creature tribal cards to be release.

20/01/2014

Five new cards have been added today. This includes the 5 color bestow beast, Chromanticore. As you'd expect from a five colored creature, it takes abilities from each of the colors. Although its a cool card I imagine it'll be very difficult to use in a draft.

The most powerful card revealed today is the Spirit of the Labyrinth. A 3/1 for 2 mana provides will provide a great boost for white weenie decks. The power alone would make the card decent but it also comes with an ability which prevents players drawing more than 1 card a turn. This has the potential to cause issues for control decks. Most control decks would have plenty of ways to deal with a 1 toughness creature so how useful this creature will be remains to be seen.

18/01/2014

MTG Mirror is now live! I've got all of the known BNG cards uploaded for you to browse. Check out more information about me and the website in the About section.

Wizards of the Coast have currently only revealed 54 cards. This means the drafting system doesn't currently make a lot of sense with only two commons revealed. As Wizards spoil more cards this facility will continue to improve.

The new cards include some new mechanics such as 'Inspired' which triggers when a creature untaps and the very interesting 'Tribute' where your opponent can put a number of +1/+1 counters on the creature or the creature performs another action such as fighting players or gaining haste. I'm a big fan of mechanics that force players to make difficult decisions and I'm excited to see some powerful creatures with this ability.

Theres also been a number of duel coloured Gods revealed including the White/Blue God, the Green/White God, the Red/Black God and the Red/Green God. We're still waiting for the god of my favourite color combo, Black/Blue.
